Services

We design, develop and maintain your website. All our packages include a domain name, web host and monthly web maintainance.

Responsive

Our websites are optimized for all screen sizes. With technology changing and improving so rapidly, it is critical for your website to be accessible via all devices, regardless of their screen size.

User Friendly

Making your website user friendly is one of the aspects we focus on most. Having a website that is not user friendly can very easily drive away potential customers. Our websites are easy to use and easy to navigate through.

Monthly Maintainance

On a monthly basis we scan your website for any security faults, bugs and errors, and terminate any we find. We will also update the site where necessary. You will be contacted in advance before the monthly maintainance to discuss what you want changed and updated.

Monthly reporting

You will receive a full statistics report on a monthly basis. With this report, you will see detailed stats e.g. visitors, page hits, time spent on page etc, with demographics of each stat e.g. Location, device, time etc.
